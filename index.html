The series focuses on Monkey D. Luffy, a young man made of rubber, who, inspired by his childhood idol, the powerful pirate Red-Haired Shanks, sets off on a journey from the East Blue Sea to find the mythical treasure, the One Piece, and proclaim himself the King of the Pirates.

Doflamingo

  • One of the most important characters of the series
  • Ate the Ito Ito no Mi devil fruit
  • Can control strings to slice his opponents

Ace

  • Luffy's older brother
  • Biological son of the late pirate king
  • Sacrificed his life to protect Luffy from the Marines

Shanks

  • Chief of the Red Hair pirates
  • He gave Luffy the famous straw hat
  • One of the four emperors of the New World
